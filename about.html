About

Brief History

With 15 years of experience in technology and the latest 10 with the .Net stack, I’m a Sr. Software Engineer having worked in complex industries such as finance, e-commerce, billing, insurance and telecom. I hold Microsoft, ScrumMaster (Scrum.org) and Sitecore certifications and am always looking for knowledge and new challenges.

I currently live and work in sunny Alberta, Canada but have previously worked in the US, Latin America and participated in projets also covering the APAC region.

My main objective is always learning new tech and updating my skills with the latest technology usually doing software architecture, development, analysis and security for Web/Mobile and Cloud environments.

I'm always on continuous improvement mode and the above information may be out of date =)
